# RoCitizen Support

Welcome to RoCitizen support! This page provides help for the Romanian citizenship quiz application.

## About RoCitizen

RoCitizen is a quiz application designed to help you study for Romanian citizenship tests. The app includes questions across multiple categories:

- Constitution (Constituție)
- Culture (Cultură)
- Geography (Geografie)
- History (Istorie)

## How to Use the App

1. **Select a Category**: Choose which topic you want to study (or "Toate" for all categories)
2. **Choose Question Count**: Select how many questions you want in your quiz (5, 10, 15, 20, or 30)
3. **Start the Quiz**: Tap "Începe testul" to begin
4. **Answer Questions**: Select your answer from the multiple choice options
5. **View Results**: After completing all questions, see your score

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How are questions selected?

Questions are randomly selected from the chosen category each time you start a new quiz.

### Can I review incorrect answers?

Currently, the app shows your final score. We recommend retaking quizzes to practice questions you may have missed.

### Is internet required?

No, all questions are stored locally in the app. You can use RoCitizen completely offline.

### How often is the content updated?

The question database is based on official Romanian citizenship test materials and is updated as needed.
